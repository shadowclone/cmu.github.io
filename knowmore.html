Club-22 Presents

CodeMeetUp

April Edition

About Club-22: Club-22 was founded in February 2022 to carry out some programming and development activities for the CSE branch.
About CodeMeetUp: This is the first event in the college since it reopened. This event is essentially an open hackathon with the goal of improving interaction among college students while learning a thing or two about web development.
Some Important details CodeMeetUp:

  • The hackathon will most likely be held on a college campus from April 21 to April 23.
  • This is a team event where teams can be of size 2-4.
  • Participants must be present in college for 3 days for 5 hours/day.
  • For developement of the project the use of the internet, as well as any other resource, is encouraged.
  • The hackathon should result in a completed project of any difficulty level.
  • Teams could be formed from students from various semester upon mutual concent.
  • Kindly read the instructions for filling the form very carefully.
  • The majority of communication will take place in a pre-existing WhatsApp group. Join.
  • Have Fun!!
